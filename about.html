Our Story

Dastoor by Khalida Khan is more than just a boutique — it's a reflection of timeless tradition, graceful femininity, and modern elegance. Founded with the vision of celebrating South Asian heritage through design, Dastoor brings together carefully crafted pieces that tell a story of cultural pride and artisanal beauty.

From bridal lehengas to festive shararas, every collection at Dastoor is curated with attention to detail, fine fabrics, and intricate embroidery that echo the traditions of our ancestors — reimagined for today’s confident woman.

With an unwavering commitment to quality, style, and individuality, our creations are designed to make you feel empowered, beautiful, and connected to your roots.

Thank you for being part of our journey.
